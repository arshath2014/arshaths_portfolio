About

Sai Abhyankkar (born 4 November 2004) is an Indian composer and singer who works primarily in Tamil films. He is the son of singers Tippu and Harini. Abhyankkar's debut independent single "Katchi Sera", composed for Think Indie, went viral across the globe and emerged as one of the most searched songs of 2024 worldwide.[1] Bakkiyaraj Kannan signed him to compose music for Benz in 2024, which was his debut in Tamil cinema, and he soon after signed on Suriya 45.
